1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout start rate for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 60-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag thermometer visibility' on Banana Republic Shopping Bag (Android Native) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 0.28% on Checkout Start Rate because a persistent thermometer educates the shopper on the free-ship benefit and raises aos.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Banana Republic Android Native.
  • Challenger: Bag thermometer visibility variant — a persistent thermometer educates the shopper on the free-ship benefit and raises aos.
