1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy Account, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Loyalty progress widget' on Old Navy Account (Promo SMS) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.65% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because a progress widget toward the next loyalty tier raises return-visit rate among members.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Account experience on Old Navy Promo SMS.
  • Challenger: Loyalty progress widget variant — a progress widget toward the next loyalty tier raises return-visit rate among members.
