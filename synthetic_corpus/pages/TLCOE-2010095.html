1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aos for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 150-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Sticky PDP ATB' on Gap PDP (iOS Native) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.8% on AOS because a sticky atb on mobile pdp keeps the primary cta visible during long scrolls.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Gap iOS Native.
  • Challenger: Sticky PDP ATB variant — a sticky atb on mobile pdp keeps the primary cta visible during long scrolls.
