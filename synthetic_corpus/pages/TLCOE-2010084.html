1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support opv conversion rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 21-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Hero single vs carousel' on Gap Homepage (Desktop) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.32% on OPV Conversion Rate because testing 70% off site-wide as part of a single curated hero outperforms a 4-slide carousel on click-through to pdp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Gap Desktop.
  • Challenger: Hero single vs carousel variant — testing 70% off site-wide as part of a single curated hero outperforms a 4-slide carousel on click-through to pdp.
