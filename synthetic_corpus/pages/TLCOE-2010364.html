1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support opv conversion rate for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP promo countdown' on Gap PLP (Mobile Web) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 0.36% on OPV Conversion Rate because testing 50% off site-wide as part of a visible countdown on promo plps increases urgency and drives session-level conversion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Gap Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: PLP promo countdown variant — testing 50% off site-wide as part of a visible countdown on promo plps increases urgency and drives session-level conversion.
