1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support email capture rate for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Personalised rail' on Gap Homepage (Mobile Web) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.31% on Email Capture Rate because an ml-ranked category rail tailored to the visitor's prior browsing improves ctr to plp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Gap Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: Personalised rail variant — an ml-ranked category rail tailored to the visitor's prior browsing improves ctr to plp.
