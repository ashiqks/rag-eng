1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aur for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Hero single vs carousel' on Gap Factory Homepage (Desktop) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.89% on AUR because a single curated hero outperforms a 4-slide carousel on click-through to pdp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Gap Factory Desktop.
  • Challenger: Hero single vs carousel variant — a single curated hero outperforms a 4-slide carousel on click-through to pdp.
