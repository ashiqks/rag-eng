1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aur for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP swatches' on Gap Factory PLP (iOS Native) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.62% on AUR because showing colour swatches on the plp tile lets shoppers find their preferred colour without entering pdp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Gap Factory iOS Native.
  • Challenger: PLP swatches variant — showing colour swatches on the plp tile lets shoppers find their preferred colour without entering pdp.
