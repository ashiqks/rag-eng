1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aur for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Sticky PDP ATB' on Banana Republic Factory PDP (Promo Email) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 0.93% on AUR because a sticky atb on mobile pdp keeps the primary cta visible during long scrolls.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Banana Republic Factory Promo Email.
  • Challenger: Sticky PDP ATB variant — a sticky atb on mobile pdp keeps the primary cta visible during long scrolls.
