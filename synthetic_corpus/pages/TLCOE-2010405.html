1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support upt for the new visitors segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 150-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Homepage limited-time banner' on Athleta Homepage (Mobile Web) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.64% on UPT because a 24-hour banner promo lifts traffic to plp during sale events.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Athleta Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: Homepage limited-time banner variant — a 24-hour banner promo lifts traffic to plp during sale events.
