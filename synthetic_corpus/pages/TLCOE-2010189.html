1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support opv conversion rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -5-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP module reorder' on Banana Republic Factory PDP (Tablet Web) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.42% on OPV Conversion Rate because moving 'you may also like' above 'recently viewed' on pdp exposes more new products and lifts cross-sell.
