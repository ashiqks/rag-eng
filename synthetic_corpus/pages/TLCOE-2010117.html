1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Account, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout start rate for the all customers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Loyalty progress widget' on Banana Republic Factory Account (iOS Native) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.41% on Checkout Start Rate because a progress widget toward the next loyalty tier raises return-visit rate among members.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Account experience on Banana Republic Factory iOS Native.
  • Challenger: Loyalty progress widget variant — a progress widget toward the next loyalty tier raises return-visit rate among members.
