1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aur for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 60-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Checkout EDD' on Old Navy Checkout (Tablet Web) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 1.42% on AUR because surfacing the edd at checkout reduces last-minute drop-off.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Old Navy Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Checkout EDD variant — surfacing the edd at checkout reduces last-minute drop-off.
