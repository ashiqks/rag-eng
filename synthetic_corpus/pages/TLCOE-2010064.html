1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Search, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout start rate for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 60-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Search autosuggest images' on Gap Search (Android Native) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.0% on Checkout Start Rate because adding product thumbnails to autosuggest improves ctr to pdp from search.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Search experience on Gap Android Native.
  • Challenger: Search autosuggest images variant — adding product thumbnails to autosuggest improves ctr to pdp from search.
