1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -56-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Checkout inline validation' on Banana Republic Factory Checkout (Android Native) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.02% on Add-to-Bag Rate because validating fields on blur (vs on submit) reduces error-bounce.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Banana Republic Factory Android Native.
  • Challenger: Checkout inline validation variant — validating fields on blur (vs on submit) reduces error-bounce.
