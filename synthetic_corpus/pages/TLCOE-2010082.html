1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout start rate for the loyalty members segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 60-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag inline gift card' on Gap Factory Shopping Bag (Tablet Web) to loyalty members, then we expect a lift of at least 1.32% on Checkout Start Rate because allowing gift-card entry inline in the bag (vs. checkout) reduces drop-off.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Gap Factory Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Bag inline gift card variant — allowing gift-card entry inline in the bag (vs. checkout) reduces drop-off.
