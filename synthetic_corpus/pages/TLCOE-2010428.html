1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP hero size' on Banana Republic PLP (iOS Native) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.1% on Checkout Completion Rate because a larger above-the-fold hero on plp improves brand cohesion and lifts engagement.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Banana Republic iOS Native.
  • Challenger: PLP hero size variant — a larger above-the-fold hero on plp improves brand cohesion and lifts engagement.
