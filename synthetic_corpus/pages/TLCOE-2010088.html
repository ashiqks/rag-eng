1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aos for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -7-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP infinite scroll' on Gap PLP (iOS Native) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.98% on AOS because infinite scroll reduces friction in browsing and lifts products viewed per visit.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Gap iOS Native.
  • Challenger: PLP infinite scroll variant — infinite scroll reduces friction in browsing and lifts products viewed per visit.
