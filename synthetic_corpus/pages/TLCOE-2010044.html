1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout start rate for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP module reorder' on Athleta PDP (iOS Native) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.62% on Checkout Start Rate because moving 'you may also like' above 'recently viewed' on pdp exposes more new products and lifts cross-sell.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Athleta iOS Native.
  • Challenger: PDP module reorder variant — moving 'you may also like' above 'recently viewed' on pdp exposes more new products and lifts cross-sell.
