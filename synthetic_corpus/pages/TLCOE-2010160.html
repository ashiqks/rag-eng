1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support opv conversion rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 28-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP hero size' on Banana Republic Factory PLP (Android Native) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.37% on OPV Conversion Rate because a larger above-the-fold hero on plp improves brand cohesion and lifts engagement.

1.3 Experimental Changes
