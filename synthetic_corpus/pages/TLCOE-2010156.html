1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 28-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP grid density' on Gap PLP (Mobile Web) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 1.18% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because increasing product density on plp improves visibility of more skus and lifts add-to-bag.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Gap Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: PLP grid density variant — increasing product density on plp improves visibility of more skus and lifts add-to-bag.
