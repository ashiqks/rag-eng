1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the loyalty members segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag auto promo' on Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag (Desktop) to loyalty members, then we expect a lift of at least 0.39% on Net RPV because automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Banana Republic Factory Desktop.
  • Challenger: Bag auto promo variant — automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.
