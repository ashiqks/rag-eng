1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout start rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 28-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Hero single vs carousel' on Old Navy Homepage (Tablet Web) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.86% on Checkout Start Rate because testing free shipping on $50+ as part of a single curated hero outperforms a 4-slide carousel on click-through to pdp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Old Navy Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Hero single vs carousel variant — testing free shipping on $50+ as part of a single curated hero outperforms a 4-slide carousel on click-through to pdp.
