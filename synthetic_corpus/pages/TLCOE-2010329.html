1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag auto promo' on Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag (Tablet Web) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.55% on Checkout Completion Rate because testing free shipping on $50+ as part of automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Banana Republic Factory Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Bag auto promo variant — testing free shipping on $50+ as part of automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.
