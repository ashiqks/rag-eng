1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aos for the new visitors segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP infinite scroll' on Athleta PLP (Triggered SMS) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.68% on AOS because infinite scroll reduces friction in browsing and lifts products viewed per visit.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Athleta Triggered SMS.
  • Challenger: PLP infinite scroll variant — infinite scroll reduces friction in browsing and lifts products viewed per visit.
