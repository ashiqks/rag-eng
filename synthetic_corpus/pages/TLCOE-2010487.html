1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support email capture rate for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -125-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Email bubble vs pop-up' on Old Navy Homepage (Android Native) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 0.74% on Email Capture Rate because a bubble email capture is less intrusive than a pop-up and improves long-term sentiment without sacrificing capture rate.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Old Navy Android Native.
  • Challenger: Email bubble vs pop-up variant — a bubble email capture is less intrusive than a pop-up and improves long-term sentiment without sacrificing capture rate.
