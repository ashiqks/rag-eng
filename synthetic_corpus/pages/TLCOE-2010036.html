1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Account, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 30-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Auto address' on Banana Republic Factory Account (Mobile Web) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.93% on Checkout Completion Rate because auto-populating the most-used saved address speeds up checkout starts.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Account experience on Banana Republic Factory Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: Auto address variant — auto-populating the most-used saved address speeds up checkout starts.
