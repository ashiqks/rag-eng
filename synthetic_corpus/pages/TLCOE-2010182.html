1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the all customers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -157-day window.

If we apply 'Bag auto promo' on Old Navy Shopping Bag (iOS Native) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.3% on Net RPV because testing 80% off site-wide as part of automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.
