1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support opv conversion rate for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 21-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag free-ship thermometer' on Old Navy Shopping Bag (Tablet Web) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.39% on OPV Conversion Rate because showing a 'add $x for free shipping' progress bar lifts aos.

1.3 Experimental Changes
