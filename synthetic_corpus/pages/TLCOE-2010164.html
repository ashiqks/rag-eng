1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the all customers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 150-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Email bubble vs pop-up' on Gap Factory Homepage (iOS Native) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.69% on Checkout Completion Rate because a bubble email capture is less intrusive than a pop-up and improves long-term sentiment without sacrificing capture rate.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Gap Factory iOS Native.
  • Challenger: Email bubble vs pop-up variant — a bubble email capture is less intrusive than a pop-up and improves long-term sentiment without sacrificing capture rate.

2. Test Results

2.1 Findings (TL;DR)

Across the 150-day test window, no statistically significant difference was observed on the primary KPI (Checkout Completion Rate); observed delta -0.3% sits within MDE (0.69%). Net RPV -0.28%, AOS 0.17% — also within noise.
