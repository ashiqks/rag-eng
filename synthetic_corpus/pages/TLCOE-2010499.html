1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aur for the loyalty members segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Homepage limited-time banner' on Old Navy Homepage (Android Native) to loyalty members, then we expect a lift of at least 0.53% on AUR because a 24-hour banner promo lifts traffic to plp during sale events.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Old Navy Android Native.
  • Challenger: Homepage limited-time banner variant — a 24-hour banner promo lifts traffic to plp during sale events.
