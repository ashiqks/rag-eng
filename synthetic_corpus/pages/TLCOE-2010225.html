1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -61-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Sticky PDP ATB' on Old Navy PDP (Android Native) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.1% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because a sticky atb on mobile pdp keeps the primary cta visible during long scrolls.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Challenger: Sticky PDP ATB variant — a sticky atb on mobile pdp keeps the primary cta visible during long scrolls.
