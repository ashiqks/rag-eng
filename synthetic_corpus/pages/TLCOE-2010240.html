1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP free-ship reminder' on Banana Republic Factory PDP (Tablet Web) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 1.21% on Checkout Completion Rate because reminding the shopper of the free-ship threshold on the pdp raises aos and conversion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Banana Republic Factory Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: PDP free-ship reminder variant — reminding the shopper of the free-ship threshold on the pdp raises aos and conversion.
