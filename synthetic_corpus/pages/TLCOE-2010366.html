1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout start rate for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 14-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP promo countdown' on Banana Republic PLP (Mobile Web) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.34% on Checkout Start Rate because testing free shipping on $50+ as part of a visible countdown on promo plps increases urgency and drives session-level conversion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Banana Republic Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: PLP promo countdown variant — testing free shipping on $50+ as part of a visible countdown on promo plps increases urgency and drives session-level conversion.
