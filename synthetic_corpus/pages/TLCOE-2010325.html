1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the all customers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 21-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Guest checkout default' on Athleta Checkout (Mobile Web) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.36% on Net RPV because defaulting to guest checkout (with optional signup at the end) lifts checkout completion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Athleta Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: Guest checkout default variant — defaulting to guest checkout (with optional signup at the end) lifts checkout completion.
