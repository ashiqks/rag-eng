1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 120-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP affinity sort' on Old Navy PLP (Desktop) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.42% on Add-to-Bag Rate because ml-ranked sort using prior browsing affinity should outperform default 'featured' sort.
