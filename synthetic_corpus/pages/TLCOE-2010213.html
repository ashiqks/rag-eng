1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support opv conversion rate for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 21-day window.

If we apply 'Checkout EDD' on Banana Republic Checkout (Loyalty Email) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.04% on OPV Conversion Rate because surfacing the edd at checkout reduces last-minute drop-off.
