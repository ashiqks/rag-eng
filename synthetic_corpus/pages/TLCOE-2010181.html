1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the all customers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP AI fit' on Banana Republic Factory PDP (iOS Native) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.26% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because an ai-driven fit recommender (à la zara) reduces size returns and lifts atb.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Banana Republic Factory iOS Native.
  • Challenger: PDP AI fit variant — an ai-driven fit recommender (à la zara) reduces size returns and lifts atb.
