1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Account, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the all customers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 32-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Auto address' on Gap Account (iOS Native) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.73% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because auto-populating the most-used saved address speeds up checkout starts.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Account experience on Gap iOS Native.
  • Challenger: Auto address variant — auto-populating the most-used saved address speeds up checkout starts.
