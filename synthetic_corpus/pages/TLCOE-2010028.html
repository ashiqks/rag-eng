1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aos for the new visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 150-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag payment collapse' on Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag (Android Native) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.74% on AOS because collapsing the alternative payment options on ios bag reduces visual clutter and lifts checkout-start.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Banana Republic Factory Android Native.
  • Challenger: Bag payment collapse variant — collapsing the alternative payment options on ios bag reduces visual clutter and lifts checkout-start.
