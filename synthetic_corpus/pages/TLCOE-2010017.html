1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support opv conversion rate for the first-time buyers segment. If we apply 'PDP AI fit' on Banana Republic Factory PDP (iOS Native) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.34% on OPV Conversion Rate because an ai-driven fit recommender (à la zara) reduces size returns and lifts atb.
