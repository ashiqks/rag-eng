1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Checkout EDD' on Gap Checkout (Loyalty Email) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.57% on Checkout Completion Rate because surfacing the edd at checkout reduces last-minute drop-off.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Gap Loyalty Email.
  • Challenger: Checkout EDD variant — surfacing the edd at checkout reduces last-minute drop-off.
