1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the all customers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -95-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP swatches' on Banana Republic Factory PLP (Tablet Web) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.38% on Net RPV because showing colour swatches on the plp tile lets shoppers find their preferred colour without entering pdp.
