1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 120-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP points estimator' on Athleta PDP (Android Native) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.47% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because showing 'you'll earn x points' on pdp reinforces loyalty value and lifts conversion among members.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Athleta Android Native.
  • Challenger: PDP points estimator variant — showing 'you'll earn x points' on pdp reinforces loyalty value and lifts conversion among members.
