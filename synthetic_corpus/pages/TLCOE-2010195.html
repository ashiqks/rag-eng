1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support upt for the new visitors segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 21-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag thermometer visibility' on Athleta Shopping Bag (Tablet Web) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.54% on UPT because a persistent thermometer educates the shopper on the free-ship benefit and raises aos.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Athleta Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Bag thermometer visibility variant — a persistent thermometer educates the shopper on the free-ship benefit and raises aos.

2.1 Findings (TL;DR)

Across the 21-day test window, the challenger outperformed the control on the primary KPI (UPT (Units Per Transaction)) by 5.04% (statistically significant at the configured threshold). Net RPV moved by 4.05% and AOS by 1.39%.
