1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aos for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Wallet pay promotion' on Banana Republic Factory Checkout (Desktop) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 0.66% on AOS because promoting wallet pay above the billing form shortens checkout for mobile users.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Banana Republic Factory Desktop.
  • Challenger: Wallet pay promotion variant — promoting wallet pay above the billing form shortens checkout for mobile users.
