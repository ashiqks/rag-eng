1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aos for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -135-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP grid density' on Banana Republic Factory PLP (iOS Native) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 0.41% on AOS because increasing product density on plp improves visibility of more skus and lifts add-to-bag.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Banana Republic Factory iOS Native.
  • Challenger: PLP grid density variant — increasing product density on plp improves visibility of more skus and lifts add-to-bag.
