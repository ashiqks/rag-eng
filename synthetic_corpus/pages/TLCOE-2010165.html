1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 28-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP infinite scroll' on Athleta PLP (Desktop) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.64% on Net RPV because infinite scroll reduces friction in browsing and lifts products viewed per visit.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Athleta Desktop.
  • Challenger: PLP infinite scroll variant — infinite scroll reduces friction in browsing and lifts products viewed per visit.
