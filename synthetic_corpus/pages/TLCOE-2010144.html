1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support email capture rate for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 34-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP promo countdown' on Gap Factory PLP (iOS Push) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.38% on Email Capture Rate because testing free shipping on $25+ as part of a visible countdown on promo plps increases urgency and drives session-level conversion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Gap Factory iOS Push.
  • Challenger: PLP promo countdown variant — testing free shipping on $25+ as part of a visible countdown on promo plps increases urgency and drives session-level conversion.
