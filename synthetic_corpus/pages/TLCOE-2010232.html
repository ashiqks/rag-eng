1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support email capture rate for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 150-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP swatches' on Old Navy PLP (Desktop) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 0.28% on Email Capture Rate because showing colour swatches on the plp tile lets shoppers find their preferred colour without entering pdp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Old Navy Desktop.
  • Challenger: PLP swatches variant — showing colour swatches on the plp tile lets shoppers find their preferred colour without entering pdp.
