1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP UGC' on Gap Factory PDP (iOS Native) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 0.81% on Checkout Completion Rate because adding ugc photos to the pdp gallery improves perceived authenticity and atb.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Gap Factory iOS Native.
  • Challenger: PDP UGC variant — adding ugc photos to the pdp gallery improves perceived authenticity and atb.
