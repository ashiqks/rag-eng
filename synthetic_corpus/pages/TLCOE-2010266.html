1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Checkout NDD countdown' on Athleta Checkout (Tablet Web) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.54% on Add-to-Bag Rate because a countdown to the next-day delivery cutoff drives urgency at checkout.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Athleta Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Checkout NDD countdown variant — a countdown to the next-day delivery cutoff drives urgency at checkout.
