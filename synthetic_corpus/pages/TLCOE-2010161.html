1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 120-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP scarcity badge' on Gap Factory PLP (Mobile Web) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.61% on Add-to-Bag Rate because surfacing low-inventory signals on plp triggers loss-aversion and lifts ctr to pdp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Gap Factory Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: PLP scarcity badge variant — surfacing low-inventory signals on plp triggers loss-aversion and lifts ctr to pdp.
