1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Account, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aur for the loyalty members segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 28-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Loyalty progress widget' on Athleta Account (Tablet Web) to loyalty members, then we expect a lift of at least 1.33% on AUR because a progress widget toward the next loyalty tier raises return-visit rate among members.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Account experience on Athleta Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Loyalty progress widget variant — a progress widget toward the next loyalty tier raises return-visit rate among members.
