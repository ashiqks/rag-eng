1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 120-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP swatches' on Gap PLP (Desktop) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.32% on Add-to-Bag Rate because showing colour swatches on the plp tile lets shoppers find their preferred colour without entering pdp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Gap Desktop.
  • Challenger: PLP swatches variant — showing colour swatches on the plp tile lets shoppers find their preferred colour without entering pdp.
