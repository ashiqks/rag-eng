1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -38-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag inline gift card' on Gap Shopping Bag (Android Native) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 0.81% on Net RPV because allowing gift-card entry inline in the bag (vs. checkout) reduces drop-off.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Gap Android Native.
  • Challenger: Bag inline gift card variant — allowing gift-card entry inline in the bag (vs. checkout) reduces drop-off.
