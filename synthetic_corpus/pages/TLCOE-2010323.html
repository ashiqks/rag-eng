1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP UGC' on Banana Republic Factory PDP (Tablet Web) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.09% on Net RPV because adding ugc photos to the pdp gallery improves perceived authenticity and atb.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Banana Republic Factory Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: PDP UGC variant — adding ugc photos to the pdp gallery improves perceived authenticity and atb.
