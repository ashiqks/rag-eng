1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support upt for the all customers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 14-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP hero size' on Gap Factory PLP (Promo Email) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.18% on UPT because a larger above-the-fold hero on plp improves brand cohesion and lifts engagement.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Challenger: PLP hero size variant — a larger above-the-fold hero on plp improves brand cohesion and lifts engagement.
