1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the loyalty members segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -229-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Checkout inline validation' on Gap Checkout (Android Native) to loyalty members, then we expect a lift of at least 0.93% on Add-to-Bag Rate because validating fields on blur (vs on submit) reduces error-bounce.
