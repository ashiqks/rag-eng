1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aos for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag auto promo' on Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag (Tablet Web) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 0.52% on AOS because testing 50% off site-wide as part of automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Banana Republic Factory Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Bag auto promo variant — testing 50% off site-wide as part of automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.
