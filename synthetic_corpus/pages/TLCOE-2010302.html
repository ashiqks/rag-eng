1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the loyalty members segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Email bubble vs pop-up' on Gap Factory Homepage (Mobile Web) to loyalty members, then we expect a lift of at least 1.0% on Checkout Completion Rate because a bubble email capture is less intrusive than a pop-up and improves long-term sentiment without sacrificing capture rate.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Gap Factory Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: Email bubble vs pop-up variant — a bubble email capture is less intrusive than a pop-up and improves long-term sentiment without sacrificing capture rate.
