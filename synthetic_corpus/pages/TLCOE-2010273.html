1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout start rate for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -219-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP scarcity badge' on Banana Republic Factory PLP (Tablet Web) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.31% on Checkout Start Rate because surfacing low-inventory signals on plp triggers loss-aversion and lifts ctr to pdp.
