1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -128-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Guest checkout default' on Old Navy Checkout (Triggered Email) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 0.27% on Net RPV because defaulting to guest checkout (with optional signup at the end) lifts checkout completion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Old Navy Triggered Email.
  • Challenger: Guest checkout default variant — defaulting to guest checkout (with optional signup at the end) lifts checkout completion.
