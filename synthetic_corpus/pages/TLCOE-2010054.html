1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout start rate for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag thermometer visibility' on Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag (Mobile Web) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.04% on Checkout Start Rate because a persistent thermometer educates the shopper on the free-ship benefit and raises aos.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Banana Republic Factory Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: Bag thermometer visibility variant — a persistent thermometer educates the shopper on the free-ship benefit and raises aos.
