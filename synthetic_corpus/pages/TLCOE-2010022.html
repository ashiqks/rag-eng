1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support email capture rate for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 120-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP scarcity label' on Gap Factory PDP (iOS Native) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 0.24% on Email Capture Rate because a scarcity label on pdp triggers urgency and lifts atb rate.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Gap Factory iOS Native.
  • Challenger: PDP scarcity label variant — a scarcity label on pdp triggers urgency and lifts atb rate.
