1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -199-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP module reorder' on Gap PDP (Android Push) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.79% on Checkout Completion Rate because moving 'you may also like' above 'recently viewed' on pdp exposes more new products and lifts cross-sell.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Gap Android Push.
  • Challenger: PDP module reorder variant — moving 'you may also like' above 'recently viewed' on pdp exposes more new products and lifts cross-sell.
