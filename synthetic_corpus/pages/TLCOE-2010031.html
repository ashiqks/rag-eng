1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 28-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Sticky PDP ATB' on Gap Factory PDP (Promo Email) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 1.04% on Add-to-Bag Rate because a sticky atb on mobile pdp keeps the primary cta visible during long scrolls.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Gap Factory Promo Email.
  • Challenger: Sticky PDP ATB variant — a sticky atb on mobile pdp keeps the primary cta visible during long scrolls.
