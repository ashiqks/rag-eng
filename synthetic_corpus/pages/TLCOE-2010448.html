1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support email capture rate for the loyalty members segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag free-ship thermometer' on Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag (iOS Native) to loyalty members, then we expect a lift of at least 0.37% on Email Capture Rate because showing a 'add $x for free shipping' progress bar lifts aos.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Banana Republic Factory iOS Native.
  • Challenger: Bag free-ship thermometer variant — showing a 'add $x for free shipping' progress bar lifts aos.
