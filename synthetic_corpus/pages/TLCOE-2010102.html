1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aur for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag payment collapse' on Athleta Shopping Bag (Triggered SMS) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 0.96% on AUR because collapsing the alternative payment options on ios bag reduces visual clutter and lifts checkout-start.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Athleta Triggered SMS.
  • Challenger: Bag payment collapse variant — collapsing the alternative payment options on ios bag reduces visual clutter and lifts checkout-start.
