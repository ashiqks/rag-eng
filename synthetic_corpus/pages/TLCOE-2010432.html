1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 28-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Single-page checkout' on Banana Republic Checkout (Mobile Web) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.62% on Net RPV because a single-page checkout reduces perceived steps and lifts completion rate.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Banana Republic Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: Single-page checkout variant — a single-page checkout reduces perceived steps and lifts completion rate.
