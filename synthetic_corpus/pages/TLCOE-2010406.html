1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout start rate for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP free-ship reminder' on Gap Factory PDP (Desktop) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.79% on Checkout Start Rate because reminding the shopper of the free-ship threshold on the pdp raises aos and conversion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Gap Factory Desktop.
  • Challenger: PDP free-ship reminder variant — reminding the shopper of the free-ship threshold on the pdp raises aos and conversion.
