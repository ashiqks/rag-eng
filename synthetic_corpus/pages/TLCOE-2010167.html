1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -35-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP free-ship reminder' on Gap Factory PDP (Mobile Web) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.91% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because reminding the shopper of the free-ship threshold on the pdp raises aos and conversion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Gap Factory Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: PDP free-ship reminder variant — reminding the shopper of the free-ship threshold on the pdp raises aos and conversion.
