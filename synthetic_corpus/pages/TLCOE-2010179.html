1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag free-ship thermometer' on Athleta Shopping Bag (iOS Native) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 0.92% on Net RPV because showing a 'add $x for free shipping' progress bar lifts aos.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Challenger: Bag free-ship thermometer variant — showing a 'add $x for free shipping' progress bar lifts aos.
