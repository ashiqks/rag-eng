1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Account, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the new visitors segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 150-day window.

If we apply 'Loyalty progress widget' on Athleta Account (Android Native) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.38% on Net RPV because a progress widget toward the next loyalty tier raises return-visit rate among members.
