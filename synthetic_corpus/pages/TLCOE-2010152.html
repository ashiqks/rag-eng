1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 150-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP promo countdown' on Old Navy PLP (Desktop) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.46% on Add-to-Bag Rate because testing stack: 30% + extra 10% as part of a visible countdown on promo plps increases urgency and drives session-level conversion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Old Navy Desktop.
  • Challenger: PLP promo countdown variant — testing stack: 30% + extra 10% as part of a visible countdown on promo plps increases urgency and drives session-level conversion.
