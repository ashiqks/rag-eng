1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aos for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Checkout NDD countdown' on Gap Factory Checkout (Tablet Web) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 0.54% on AOS because a countdown to the next-day delivery cutoff drives urgency at checkout.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Gap Factory Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Checkout NDD countdown variant — a countdown to the next-day delivery cutoff drives urgency at checkout.
