1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the all customers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP grid density' on Old Navy PLP (Mobile Web) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.36% on Net RPV because increasing product density on plp improves visibility of more skus and lifts add-to-bag.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Old Navy Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: PLP grid density variant — increasing product density on plp improves visibility of more skus and lifts add-to-bag.
