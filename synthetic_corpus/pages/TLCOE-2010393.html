1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 60-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP free-ship reminder' on Gap PDP (Tablet Web) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.97% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because reminding the shopper of the free-ship threshold on the pdp raises aos and conversion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Gap Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: PDP free-ship reminder variant — reminding the shopper of the free-ship threshold on the pdp raises aos and conversion.

2. Test Results

2.1 Findings (TL;DR)

Across the 60-day test window, the challenger outperformed the control on the primary KPI (PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion) by 0.66% (statistically significant at the configured threshold). Net RPV moved by 0.7% and AOS by 1.75%.
