1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aos for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP video hero' on Banana Republic Factory PDP (Desktop) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.31% on AOS because replacing the static pdp hero with a 6-second product video improves engagement and atb.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Banana Republic Factory Desktop.
  • Challenger: PDP video hero variant — replacing the static pdp hero with a 6-second product video improves engagement and atb.
