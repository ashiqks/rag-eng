1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aur for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Sticky checkout CTA' on Gap Checkout (iOS Native) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.45% on AUR because a sticky bottom cta on mobile checkout reduces drop-off at the payment step.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Gap iOS Native.
  • Challenger: Sticky checkout CTA variant — a sticky bottom cta on mobile checkout reduces drop-off at the payment step.
