1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Checkout EDD' on Gap Factory Checkout (Promo Email) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 1.08% on Add-to-Bag Rate because surfacing the edd at checkout reduces last-minute drop-off.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Gap Factory Promo Email.
  • Challenger: Checkout EDD variant — surfacing the edd at checkout reduces last-minute drop-off.
