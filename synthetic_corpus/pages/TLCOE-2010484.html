1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory Search, the current control experience does not sufficiently support email capture rate for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 180-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Search autosuggest images' on Gap Factory Search (Desktop) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.72% on Email Capture Rate because adding product thumbnails to autosuggest improves ctr to pdp from search.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Search experience on Gap Factory Desktop.
  • Challenger: Search autosuggest images variant — adding product thumbnails to autosuggest improves ctr to pdp from search.
