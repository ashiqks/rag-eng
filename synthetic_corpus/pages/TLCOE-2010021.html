On Athleta PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aur for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 150-day window.

If we apply 'PLP hero size' on Athleta PLP (Desktop) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 1.34% on AUR because a larger above-the-fold hero on plp improves brand cohesion and lifts engagement.
