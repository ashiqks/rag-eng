1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 60-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP infinite scroll' on Banana Republic Factory PLP (Android Native) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.27% on Add-to-Bag Rate because infinite scroll reduces friction in browsing and lifts products viewed per visit.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Banana Republic Factory Android Native.
  • Challenger: PLP infinite scroll variant — infinite scroll reduces friction in browsing and lifts products viewed per visit.
