1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support opv conversion rate for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 60-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag inline gift card' on Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag (Loyalty Email) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 1.1% on OPV Conversion Rate because allowing gift-card entry inline in the bag (vs. checkout) reduces drop-off.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Banana Republic Factory Loyalty Email.
  • Challenger: Bag inline gift card variant — allowing gift-card entry inline in the bag (vs. checkout) reduces drop-off.
