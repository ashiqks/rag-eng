1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -152-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag thermometer visibility' on Banana Republic Shopping Bag (Tablet Web) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 0.28% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because a persistent thermometer educates the shopper on the free-ship benefit and raises aos.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Banana Republic Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Bag thermometer visibility variant — a persistent thermometer educates the shopper on the free-ship benefit and raises aos.
