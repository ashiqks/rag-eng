1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support opv conversion rate for the loyalty members segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -68-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Checkout NDD countdown' on Gap Factory Checkout (iOS Native) to loyalty members, then we expect a lift of at least 0.44% on OPV Conversion Rate because a countdown to the next-day delivery cutoff drives urgency at checkout.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Gap Factory iOS Native.
  • Challenger: Checkout NDD countdown variant — a countdown to the next-day delivery cutoff drives urgency at checkout.
