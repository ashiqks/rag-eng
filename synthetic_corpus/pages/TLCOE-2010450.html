1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the all customers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 180-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Personalised rail' on Banana Republic Homepage (Mobile Web) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.6% on Checkout Completion Rate because an ml-ranked category rail tailored to the visitor's prior browsing improves ctr to plp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Banana Republic Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: Personalised rail variant — an ml-ranked category rail tailored to the visitor's prior browsing improves ctr to plp.
