1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag auto promo' on Old Navy Shopping Bag (Desktop) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.68% on Checkout Completion Rate because testing free shipping on $50+ as part of automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Old Navy Desktop.
  • Challenger: Bag auto promo variant — testing free shipping on $50+ as part of automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.
