1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout start rate for the all customers segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag free-ship thermometer' on Gap Shopping Bag (Tablet Web) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.4% on Checkout Start Rate because showing a 'add $x for free shipping' progress bar lifts aos.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Gap Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Bag free-ship thermometer variant — showing a 'add $x for free shipping' progress bar lifts aos.
