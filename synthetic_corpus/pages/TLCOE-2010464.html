1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support email capture rate for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 14-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP video hero' on Gap Factory PDP (Desktop) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 0.7% on Email Capture Rate because replacing the static pdp hero with a 6-second product video improves engagement and atb.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Gap Factory Desktop.
  • Challenger: PDP video hero variant — replacing the static pdp hero with a 6-second product video improves engagement and atb.

2. Test Results

2.1 Findings (TL;DR)

Across the 14-day test window, no statistically significant difference was observed on the primary KPI (Email Capture Rate); observed delta -0.06% sits within MDE (0.7%). Net RPV 0.08%, AOS -0.1% — also within noise.
