1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Checkout NDD countdown' on Banana Republic Checkout (Triggered Email) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.77% on Checkout Completion Rate because a countdown to the next-day delivery cutoff drives urgency at checkout.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Banana Republic Triggered Email.
  • Challenger: Checkout NDD countdown variant — a countdown to the next-day delivery cutoff drives urgency at checkout.
