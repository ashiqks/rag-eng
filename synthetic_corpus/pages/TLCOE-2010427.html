1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 21-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Email bubble vs pop-up' on Athleta Homepage (Android Native) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.12% on Add-to-Bag Rate because a bubble email capture is less intrusive than a pop-up and improves long-term sentiment without sacrificing capture rate.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Athleta Android Native.
  • Challenger: Email bubble vs pop-up variant — a bubble email capture is less intrusive than a pop-up and improves long-term sentiment without sacrificing capture rate.
