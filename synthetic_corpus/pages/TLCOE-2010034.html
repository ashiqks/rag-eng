1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support email capture rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -103-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP free-ship reminder' on Gap Factory PDP (Desktop) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.16% on Email Capture Rate because reminding the shopper of the free-ship threshold on the pdp raises aos and conversion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Challenger: PDP free-ship reminder variant — reminding the shopper of the free-ship threshold on the pdp raises aos and conversion.
