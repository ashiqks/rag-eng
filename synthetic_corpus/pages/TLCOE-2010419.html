1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support opv conversion rate for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 150-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Homepage limited-time banner' on Old Navy Homepage (Android Push) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.87% on OPV Conversion Rate because a 24-hour banner promo lifts traffic to plp during sale events.

1.3 Experimental Changes
