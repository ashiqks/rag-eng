1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Search, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Search recent chips' on Banana Republic Factory Search (iOS Native) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.32% on Checkout Completion Rate because recent search chips on the search overlay reduce typing.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Search experience on Banana Republic Factory iOS Native.
  • Challenger: Search recent chips variant — recent search chips on the search overlay reduce typing.
