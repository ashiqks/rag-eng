1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 120-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Hero single vs carousel' on Banana Republic Homepage (Triggered Email) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.06% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because a single curated hero outperforms a 4-slide carousel on click-through to pdp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Banana Republic Triggered Email.
  • Challenger: Hero single vs carousel variant — a single curated hero outperforms a 4-slide carousel on click-through to pdp.
