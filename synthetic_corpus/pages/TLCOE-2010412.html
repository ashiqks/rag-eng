1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout start rate for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 180-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP infinite scroll' on Banana Republic PLP (iOS Native) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.68% on Checkout Start Rate because infinite scroll reduces friction in browsing and lifts products viewed per visit.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Banana Republic iOS Native.
  • Challenger: PLP infinite scroll variant — infinite scroll reduces friction in browsing and lifts products viewed per visit.
