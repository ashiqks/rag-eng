1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aos for the new visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 14-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP hero size' on Gap PLP (Android Native) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.94% on AOS because a larger above-the-fold hero on plp improves brand cohesion and lifts engagement.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Gap Android Native.
  • Challenger: PLP hero size variant — a larger above-the-fold hero on plp improves brand cohesion and lifts engagement.
