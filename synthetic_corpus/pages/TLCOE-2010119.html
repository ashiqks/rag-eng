1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the new visitors segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 14-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Wallet pay promotion' on Banana Republic Factory Checkout (Mobile Web) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.3% on Net RPV because promoting wallet pay above the billing form shortens checkout for mobile users.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Banana Republic Factory Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: Wallet pay promotion variant — promoting wallet pay above the billing form shortens checkout for mobile users.

2.1 Findings (TL;DR)

Across the 14-day test window, the challenger underperformed the control on the primary KPI (Net RPV) by 3.75% (statistically significant in the wrong direction). Net RPV moved by -3.86% and AOS by -1.61%.
