1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 10-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP scarcity badge' on Gap PLP (Mobile Web) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.36% on Checkout Completion Rate because surfacing low-inventory signals on plp triggers loss-aversion and lifts ctr to pdp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Gap Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: PLP scarcity badge variant — surfacing low-inventory signals on plp triggers loss-aversion and lifts ctr to pdp.
