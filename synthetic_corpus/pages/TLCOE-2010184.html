1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory Account, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Loyalty progress widget' on Gap Factory Account (Triggered SMS) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.2% on Add-to-Bag Rate because a progress widget toward the next loyalty tier raises return-visit rate among members.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Account experience on Gap Factory Triggered SMS.
  • Challenger: Loyalty progress widget variant — a progress widget toward the next loyalty tier raises return-visit rate among members.
