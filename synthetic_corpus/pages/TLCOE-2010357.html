On Old Navy Account, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 68-day window.

If we apply 'Auto address' on Old Navy Account (Desktop) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 0.88% on Net RPV because auto-populating the most-used saved address speeds up checkout starts.
