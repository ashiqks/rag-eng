1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Search, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aos for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 120-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Search autosuggest images' on Athleta Search (Mobile Web) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.16% on AOS because adding product thumbnails to autosuggest improves ctr to pdp from search.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Search experience on Athleta Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: Search autosuggest images variant — adding product thumbnails to autosuggest improves ctr to pdp from search.
