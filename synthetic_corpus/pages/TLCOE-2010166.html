1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -165-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP scarcity label' on Gap PDP (iOS Native) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 0.47% on Net RPV because a scarcity label on pdp triggers urgency and lifts atb rate.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PDP experience on Gap iOS Native.
  • Challenger: PDP scarcity label variant — a scarcity label on pdp triggers urgency and lifts atb rate.
