1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support email capture rate for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP scarcity badge' on Gap Factory PLP (Desktop) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.21% on Email Capture Rate because surfacing low-inventory signals on plp triggers loss-aversion and lifts ctr to pdp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Gap Factory Desktop.
  • Challenger: PLP scarcity badge variant — surfacing low-inventory signals on plp triggers loss-aversion and lifts ctr to pdp.
