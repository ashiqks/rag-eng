1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Old Navy Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 21-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Guest checkout default' on Old Navy Checkout (iOS Native) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.89% on Add-to-Bag Rate because defaulting to guest checkout (with optional signup at the end) lifts checkout completion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Old Navy iOS Native.
  • Challenger: Guest checkout default variant — defaulting to guest checkout (with optional signup at the end) lifts checkout completion.
