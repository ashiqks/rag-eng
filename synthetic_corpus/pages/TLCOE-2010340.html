1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory Account, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aos for the new visitors segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Auto address' on Gap Factory Account (Tablet Web) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.73% on AOS because auto-populating the most-used saved address speeds up checkout starts.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Account experience on Gap Factory Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Auto address variant — auto-populating the most-used saved address speeds up checkout starts.
