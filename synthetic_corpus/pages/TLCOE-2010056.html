1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the new visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -128-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag payment collapse' on Athleta Shopping Bag (Tablet Web) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.42% on Add-to-Bag Rate because collapsing the alternative payment options on ios bag reduces visual clutter and lifts checkout-start.
