1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support opv conversion rate for the all customers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 14-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag auto promo' on Banana Republic Factory Shopping Bag (Promo SMS) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.31% on OPV Conversion Rate because automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Banana Republic Factory Promo SMS.
  • Challenger: Bag auto promo variant — automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.
