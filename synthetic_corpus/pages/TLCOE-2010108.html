1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 91-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Checkout NDD countdown' on Banana Republic Factory Checkout (Mobile Web) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.94% on Net RPV because a countdown to the next-day delivery cutoff drives urgency at checkout.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Banana Republic Factory Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: Checkout NDD countdown variant — a countdown to the next-day delivery cutoff drives urgency at checkout.
