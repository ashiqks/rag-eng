1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 120-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Checkout EDD' on Banana Republic Factory Checkout (iOS Native) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.58% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because surfacing the edd at checkout reduces last-minute drop-off.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Banana Republic Factory iOS Native.
  • Challenger: Checkout EDD variant — surfacing the edd at checkout reduces last-minute drop-off.
