1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Sticky checkout CTA' on Banana Republic Factory Checkout (iOS Push) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.49% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because a sticky bottom cta on mobile checkout reduces drop-off at the payment step.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Banana Republic Factory iOS Push.
  • Challenger: Sticky checkout CTA variant — a sticky bottom cta on mobile checkout reduces drop-off at the payment step.
