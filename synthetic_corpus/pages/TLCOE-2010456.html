1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support opv conversion rate for the high-aov customers segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 28-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Wallet pay promotion' on Gap Checkout (Android Native) to high-aov customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.28% on OPV Conversion Rate because promoting wallet pay above the billing form shortens checkout for mobile users.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Gap Android Native.
  • Challenger: Wallet pay promotion variant — promoting wallet pay above the billing form shortens checkout for mobile users.
