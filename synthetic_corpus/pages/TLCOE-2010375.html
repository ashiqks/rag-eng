1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support email capture rate for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the mid tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Email bubble vs pop-up' on Gap Homepage (Triggered SMS) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.03% on Email Capture Rate because a bubble email capture is less intrusive than a pop-up and improves long-term sentiment without sacrificing capture rate.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Gap Triggered SMS.
  • Challenger: Email bubble vs pop-up variant — a bubble email capture is less intrusive than a pop-up and improves long-term sentiment without sacrificing capture rate.

Across the 42-day test window, the challenger outperformed the control on the primary KPI (Email Capture Rate) by 1.45% (statistically significant at the configured threshold). Net RPV moved by 1.94% and AOS by 1.08%.
