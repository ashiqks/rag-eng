1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP scarcity badge' on Banana Republic PLP (Mobile Web) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 1.25% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because surfacing low-inventory signals on plp triggers loss-aversion and lifts ctr to pdp.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Banana Republic Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: PLP scarcity badge variant — surfacing low-inventory signals on plp triggers loss-aversion and lifts ctr to pdp.
