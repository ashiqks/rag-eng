1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aur for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 14-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP promo countdown' on Banana Republic Factory PLP (iOS Native) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.23% on AUR because a visible countdown on promo plps increases urgency and drives session-level conversion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Banana Republic Factory iOS Native.
  • Challenger: PLP promo countdown variant — a visible countdown on promo plps increases urgency and drives session-level conversion.
