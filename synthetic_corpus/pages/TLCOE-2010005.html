1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Search, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aur for the new visitors segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 150-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Search recent chips' on Athleta Search (Android Push) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.36% on AUR because recent search chips on the search overlay reduce typing.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Search experience on Athleta Android Push.
  • Challenger: Search recent chips variant — recent search chips on the search overlay reduce typing.
