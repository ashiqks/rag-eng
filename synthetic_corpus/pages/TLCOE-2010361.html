1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the returning visitors segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP grid density' on Athleta PLP (Tablet Web) to returning visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.58% on Add-to-Bag Rate because increasing product density on plp improves visibility of more skus and lifts add-to-bag.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Athleta Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: PLP grid density variant — increasing product density on plp improves visibility of more skus and lifts add-to-bag.
