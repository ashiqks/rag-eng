1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the loyalty members segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Sticky PLP filters' on Banana Republic Factory PLP (Mobile Web) to loyalty members, then we expect a lift of at least 0.75% on Net RPV because a persistent filter rail removes the need to scroll back to the top, accelerating refinement and conversion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Banana Republic Factory Mobile Web.
  • Challenger: Sticky PLP filters variant — a persistent filter rail removes the need to scroll back to the top, accelerating refinement and conversion.
