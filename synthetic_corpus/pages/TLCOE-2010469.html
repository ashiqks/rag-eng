1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support checkout completion rate for the all customers segment. Based on prior urgency-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 180-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Homepage limited-time banner' on Athleta Homepage (iOS Push) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 1.37% on Checkout Completion Rate because a 24-hour banner promo lifts traffic to plp during sale events.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Homepage experience on Athleta iOS Push.
  • Challenger: Homepage limited-time banner variant — a 24-hour banner promo lifts traffic to plp during sale events.
