1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the all customers segment. Based on prior value-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the -161-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag thermometer visibility' on Athleta Shopping Bag (Mobile Web) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.96% on Add-to-Bag Rate because a persistent thermometer educates the shopper on the free-ship benefit and raises aos.

1.3 Experimental Changes
