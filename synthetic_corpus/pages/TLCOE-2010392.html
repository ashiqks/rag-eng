1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Gap Factory Shopping Bag, the current control experience does not sufficiently support net rpv for the new visitors segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 120-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Bag auto promo' on Gap Factory Shopping Bag (Loyalty Email) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.12% on Net RPV because automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Shopping Bag experience on Gap Factory Loyalty Email.
  • Challenger: Bag auto promo variant — automatically applying the best eligible promo code lifts conversion in the bag step.
