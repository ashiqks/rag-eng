1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the cart abandoners segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 60-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP hero size' on Athleta PLP (Desktop) to cart abandoners, then we expect a lift of at least 1.08% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because a larger above-the-fold hero on plp improves brand cohesion and lifts engagement.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Athleta Desktop.
  • Challenger: PLP hero size variant — a larger above-the-fold hero on plp improves brand cohesion and lifts engagement.
