1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory Search, the current control experience does not sufficiently support upt for the first-time buyers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Search autosuggest images' on Banana Republic Factory Search (Tablet Web) to first-time buyers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.2% on UPT because adding product thumbnails to autosuggest improves ctr to pdp from search.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Search experience on Banana Republic Factory Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Search autosuggest images variant — adding product thumbnails to autosuggest improves ctr to pdp from search.
