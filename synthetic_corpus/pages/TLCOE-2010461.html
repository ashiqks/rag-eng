1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Factory PDP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support email capture rate for the all customers segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the discount tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 28-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PDP AI fit' on Banana Republic Factory PDP (Tablet Web) to all customers, then we expect a lift of at least 0.4% on Email Capture Rate because an ai-driven fit recommender (à la zara) reduces size returns and lifts atb.
