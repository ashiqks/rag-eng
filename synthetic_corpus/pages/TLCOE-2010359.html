1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the new visitors segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 60-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'PLP infinite scroll' on Athleta PLP (Desktop) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 1.4% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because infinite scroll reduces friction in browsing and lifts products viewed per visit.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Athleta Desktop.
  • Challenger: PLP infinite scroll variant — infinite scroll reduces friction in browsing and lifts products viewed per visit.

2. Test Results

2.1 Findings (TL;DR)

Across the 60-day test window, the challenger underperformed the control on the primary KPI (PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion) by 3.0% (statistically significant in the wrong direction). Net RPV moved by -2.74% and AOS by -2.33%.
