1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta Checkout, the current control experience does not sufficiently support add-to-bag rate for the non-loyalty segment. Based on prior quality-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 90-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Sticky checkout CTA' on Athleta Checkout (Tablet Web) to non-loyalty, then we expect a lift of at least 0.28% on Add-to-Bag Rate because a sticky bottom cta on mobile checkout reduces drop-off at the payment step.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing Checkout experience on Athleta Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Sticky checkout CTA variant — a sticky bottom cta on mobile checkout reduces drop-off at the payment step.
