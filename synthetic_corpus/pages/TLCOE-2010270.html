1.1 Problem Statement

On Banana Republic Homepage, the current control experience does not sufficiently support aur for the loyalty members segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 42-day window.

If we apply 'Personalised rail' on Banana Republic Homepage (iOS Native) to loyalty members, then we expect a lift of at least 0.96% on AUR because an ml-ranked category rail tailored to the visitor's prior browsing improves ctr to plp.
