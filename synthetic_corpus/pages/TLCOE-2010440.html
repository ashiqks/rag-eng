1. Test Plan

1.1 Problem Statement

On Athleta PLP, the current control experience does not sufficiently support pdp -> shopping bag conversion for the new visitors segment. Based on prior time savings-tactic learnings across the premium tier, we want to evaluate whether the proposed change moves the needle within the 28-day window.

1.2 Hypothesis

If we apply 'Sticky PLP filters' on Athleta PLP (Tablet Web) to new visitors, then we expect a lift of at least 0.53% on PDP -> Shopping Bag Conversion because a persistent filter rail removes the need to scroll back to the top, accelerating refinement and conversion.

1.3 Experimental Changes

  • Control: existing PLP experience on Athleta Tablet Web.
  • Challenger: Sticky PLP filters variant — a persistent filter rail removes the need to scroll back to the top, accelerating refinement and conversion.
